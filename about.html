College Introduction

Tribhuvan University, Bhaktapur Multiple Campus (BKMC) is established in 1959 in Bhaktapur, situated at the heart of Bhaktapur Municipality, Bagmati Province, as an educational institute to educate young Nepalese students in IT, Science, Humanities and Management.

BKMC aims to produce skilled manpower in fields like Information Technology, Finance, Accounting, Management, Banking, Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics.

BKMC Aims:

(1) To develop IT professionals, scientists and managers for public and private sectors with strong technical and analytical skills.

(2) To produce socially responsible and creative entrepreneurs capable of promoting business and industry for the socio-economic development of Nepal,

(3) To conduct research programs for enhancing the knowledge and skill base of academics and practicing computer & Information Technologist, managers Scientist and Social Scientist.
